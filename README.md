# 📉 Telco Customer Churn Analysis
An end-to-end churn analytics project built on the IBM Telco Customer Dataset — from raw data cleaning to a fully interactive BI dashboard.

# 🔍 Problem Statement
Customer churn is one of the most costly problems in the telecom industry. With acquisition costs far exceeding retention costs, identifying why customers leave — and who is most at risk — is a high-value business problem.
This project analyzes 7,043 telecom subscribers to surface the key drivers of churn, quantify revenue at risk, and translate findings into actionable retention strategies.

# 🛠 Tools & Techniques
Data cleaning - Microsoft Excel

Cohort enrichment - COUNTIFS, AVERAGEIFS, IF, IFS

Segmentation - Tenure-based cohort buckets (0–72 months)

CLV modeling - Monthly charges × tenure by contract tier

Visualization - HTML + Chart.js (bar, line, donut, scatter, heatmap)

Dashboard design - Tabbed navigation, KPI cards, retention heatmap, revenue-at-risk

AI Tools. 
# 📁 Dataset

Source: IBM Telco Customer Churn — Kaggle

Records: 7,043 customers

Features: 21 columns including demographics, services subscribed, contract type, payment method, monthly charges, tenure, and churn label (binary)
# 📌 Skills Demonstrated
Exploratory data analysis (EDA)

Cohort segmentation and lifecycle analysis

Customer lifetime value (CLV) modeling

Business-framed insight communication

Interactive dashboard design and development

Data storytelling for non-technical audiences
# 📈 Dashboard Features

The interactive HTML dashboard (dashboard/telco_churn_dashboard.html) includes four tabbed views:

Overview — KPI cards, churn by contract type, churn by internet service, payment method breakdown, and overall churn donut

Cohort Analysis — Churn rate by tenure bucket (lifecycle view), cohort size distribution, and a contract × tenure retention heatmap

CLV & Revenue — CLV by contract tier, monthly charge vs tenure scatter (churned vs retained), revenue at risk by service tier

Churn Drivers — Ranked risk factor bar chart, demographic segment analysis, high-risk customer profile, and top retention strategies
